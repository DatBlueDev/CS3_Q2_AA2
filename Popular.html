Brief history on memes

Some of these are very well known, and/or has survived the trial of time and has remained relevance after a long time since its debut. the earliest meme found has been recorded to be 1921; though its prominence exploded when the internet became available to the public, and where information was easily shared and accessed all over the globe.

Never Gonna Give you Up by Rick Astley, the most popular Bait&Switch meme.

Among Us is a 2018 online multiplayer social deductive game developed and published by InnerSloth. It got a lot of fame and attention and people would say terms such as "sus", "impostor", "vent", "amogus" among others.

"Gigachad" is as meme depicting a muscular man, this gif/image is often used when saying a "based" or correct opinon.

Loss is a comic strip from 2008 which was mocked and parodied heavily for its poorly-executed drama.

Breaking Bad is a show released in 2008 about a chemistry teacher Walter White. After his lung cancer diagnosis, he "breaks bad" to provide for his family. This show was immensely successful and still has fans and relevance today.

Bad Apple!! is a Japanese Pop song and a remix from the Touhou Project from 2007. It's known for its music video using silhouette shadow art, Bad Apple!! is also usually played in unorthodox devices or places, such as minecraft pixel art, a graphing calculator, command prompt, etc.
