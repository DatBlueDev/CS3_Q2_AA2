~ Memes ~

Brief definition of memes

The Urban Dictionary defines memes as: "Popular quotes, images, and real people, which are copied, imitated, and spread all over the internet(s)!" memes are usually humorous and/or employs irony or sarcasm to deliver a message, the purpose and topics of the memes can be relatable, funny, irrelevant, political, almost any topic you can think of; it is a form of sharing and connecting.

Brief history on memes

Memes have been around for a long time now, the earliest meme found has been recorded to be 1921; though its prominence exploded when the internet became available to the public, and where information was easily shared and accessed all over the globe.
