TrashPosting: A peculiar kind of meme

What is TrashPosting?

Trashposting, or funposting, but better known as s***posting is an act of posting satirical content that is of "aggressively, ironically, or trollishly poor quality" - wikipedia. Some of this content are weird and doesn't make sense; some intentionally have poor qraphical quality to emphasize the satire.

Colonel Whatsapp, a satirical combination of Colonel Sanders of KFC, and the app Whatsapp, along with a Whatsapp themed remix. These two things have no relation to each other, this post does not make any sense at all; yet here it is.

Saul Goodman is a lawyer from the hit shows Breaking Bad and Better Call Saul. This meme represents Saul Goodman Talking to the jury that his client simply did it "for the vine." which is not a valid arguement in the law, and Saul Goodman never said this anywhere in the show, but it is still funny, I guess.

A gif of something that resembles Among Us, talking. This too doesn't makes sense, as the Among Us character does not feature a mouth, and the gif replaced it's visor with the mouth.

This video depicts a intentionally low-quality recreation of another meme "Torture Dance". The gibberish singing and the low quality of the video enhances the "viewing experience."

This meme depicts a trollface riding a cart fueled by a flashlight and going at the speed of light, disobeying the laws of physics. The reaction to this image is a popular clip from the hit show "American Psycho", asking why isn't it possible.

This meme represents an emoji construction worker telling us the meme is still being built; this in and of itself, is the meme.
